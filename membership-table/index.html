Membership Categories & Fees+

The DPJCC offers membership packages and rates to meet your individual needs.

Category Description Monthly Dues
Family* Couple and all children under 25 years of age. $123
Parent Plus* One adult and all children under 25 years of age. $103
Young Adult Individual 18–35 years of age who is not a full-time student. $67
Individual Individual 36–59 years of age. $77
Couple Couple without any children residing at home. $113
Senior Couple Couple with at least one individual 60 years of age or over. $95
Individual Senior Individual 60 years of age or older. $67
Student* Full-time student 13–25 years of age with a current valid student I.D. $59
College Student Summer/vacation memberships available. Contact the membership office.

Fees are subject to change. Effective 1/1/2026. There is a $99 registration fee.

*13 & 14-year-old members are required to take the free FitTeen class in order to utilize the fitness center. Children under the age of 13 are not permitted in the fitness center.

+If a payment is returned for any reason, there will be a $30 charge. The $30 fee and the declined monthly membership payment must be paid within 10 days of notification, or membership may be discontinued.
